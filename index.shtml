The VST Photometric Hα Survey of the Southern Galactic Plane and Bulge (VPHAS+)

Read the survey description » VPHAS+ is imaging the Southern Milky Way in visible light (u, g, r, i, Hα) at ~1 arcsec resolution to >20th magnitude using the VLT Survey Telescope (VST) in Chile.

The VST/Omegacam Photometric Hα Survey (VPHAS) is imaging the entire Southern Milky Way in visible light at ~1 arcsec angular resolution down to >20th magnitude using the VLT Survey Telescope in Chile.
Survey

VPHAS+ uses VST/OmegaCam to survey the Southern Galactic Plane and Bulge in u, g, r, i, Hα down the ~20th magnitude.
