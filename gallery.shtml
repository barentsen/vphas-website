Westerlund 1

The image below shows a VPHAS-based mosaic of the massive star cluster Westerlund 1. The VPHAS+ data towards this cluster has unveiled an ionized nebula around a red supergiant (see Wright et al. 2014), which was the subject of an RAS press release.

Credit: VPHAS+/Nick Wright.
